Funding

Open States is a collection of tools that make it possible for citizens to track what is happening in their state's capitol by aggregating information from all 50 states, Washington, D.C., and Puerto Rico. See our our about page for more information.

Donate Today

Open States serves millions of API calls and thousands of web users daily and depends upon the generosity of donors to fund our work and expenses. If you find Open States a valuable resource please consider donating today.
